introduction

Hello! If you have found this page, it means I have dropped a link to it somewhere for you, or you have scanned my forearm. Either way, welcome to this little corner callback to the early 00s, when Google was reputably not a piece of shit and the internet wasn't dead. Yes, I'm nostalgic for a past both before her time, and hardly existed in the first place. Sue me.

What is this page for? General information docket for the QR code I have or have had tattooed to my forearm, listing out various items such as:

  • Important medical information
  • Unimportant medical information
  • General blog-posting
  • A guestbook! I should make a guestbook and throw the comments up somewhere.

I don't know how HTML or CSS or JS works, but I know how to rip off other people's hard work and mold it into something I kinda sorta like, so that is what this page is going to be.
