introduction

Hello! If you have found this page, it means I have dropped a link to it somewhere for you, or you have scanned my forearm. Either way, welcome to this little corner callback to the early 00s, when Google was reputably not a piece of shit and the internet wasn't dead. Yes, I'm nostalgic for a past that I wasn't even alive for. Sue me.

So what is this page for? General information docket listing out various items such as:

  • Important medical information
  • General blog-posting
  • A flight log
  • A guestbook! Please sign it, I like knowing who's visited.

I don't know how HTML or CSS or JS works, but I know how to rip off other people's hard work and mold it into something I kinda sorta like, so that is what this page is going to be.
